About David

DRIVEN BY PASSION,
INSPIRED BY ACTION

Two-time All-American at the University of Georgia. First-round NFL Draft pick. ESPN college football analyst. These accolades only scratch the surface of who David Pollack is.

After a career-ending neck injury, David didn't retreat — he redirected. Today he channels that same competitive fire into inspiring audiences across corporate events, faith-based gatherings, and everything in between. His message is simple: every day counts.
